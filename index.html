About me

Who am I and what keeps me busy?

That's me

I am 32, married and a photography and travel enthusiast. I have a German Diplom in Information Systems from the University of Mannheim and just completed my dissertation to obtain a Doctor of Business Administration.

Together with my wife, I like to discover new places and bring home snapshots of the world's sights and landmarks.

Work

For the past 4 years, I have been working as a Research Associate at the University of Mannheim. In this role I have been busy in exploring the implications of new technologies like Cloud Computing and Software-as-a-Service on the business models and organizational structures of companies. In addition, I have been responsible for managing and initiating research projects, coordinating stakeholders like researchers, industry partners and funding organizations and managing research budgets.

Currently I am searching for the next new challenge.
